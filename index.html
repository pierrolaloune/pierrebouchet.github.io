Welcome

Welcome to my academic webpage! I am a PhD candidate specializing in ecology, conservation biology, community ecology, macroecology, and biogeography. I study the links between biodiversity, ecosystem functions, and human use patterns in the Anthropocene.

Biography

I am currently pursuing my PhD in ecology at the Université Paul Sabatier - Toulouse, under the supervision of Sébastien Brosse and Aurèle Toussaint. My doctoral research focuses on the functional diversity of vertebrates and the impacts of human use on extinction risk and biogeographical patterns.

Research Interests

  • Functional ecology
  • Biodiversity and conservation biology
  • Macroecology and biogeography
  • People and nature
  • Biodiversity and anthropocene
